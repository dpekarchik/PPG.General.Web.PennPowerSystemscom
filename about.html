Our story

Power generation is all we do.

Penn Power Systems has spent decades doing one thing: keeping critical power on for the businesses and institutions of the Mid-Atlantic and Northeast. We're a sales, rental, service, and parts company built around a simple idea — the people who install your power system should be the same people who answer the phone at 2 a.m. when it matters.

From our Philadelphia headquarters and four regional service centers, our EGSA-certified, factory-trained technicians support hospitals, data centers, water treatment plants, manufacturers, telecom sites, and the events that simply cannot go dark. We sell equipment we'll still be servicing in 2040, and we don't walk away after commissioning.
