Why it matters

Why emergency power is essential.

  • Safety — reliable power keeps lighting, security cameras, and alarm systems running, protecting occupants and responders.
  • Business continuity — uninterrupted power maintains operations, preserves data, and serves customers — outages risk losses and reputation.
  • Healthcare facilities — hospitals rely on continuous power to sustain life-saving equipment and patient care.
  • Community support — shelters, communication centers, and response units depend on power during disasters.
  • Infrastructure protection — water treatment, transportation, and telecom networks need uninterrupted power to avoid widespread disruption.
