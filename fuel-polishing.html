Why it matters

Why fuel polishing matters.

Over time, fuel stored in tanks degrades, accumulating contaminants like water, sludge, and microbial growth. These impurities clog filters, damage engine components, and reduce efficiency. With high fuel costs, fuel condition is critical — fuel that sits too long undergoes molecular deterioration, causing partial combustion or loss of power.

How it works

Our fuel polishing process.

1

Recondition

Removal of water and particulates that are easily drained from the system.

2

Stabilize

ALGAE-X fuel conditioner reverses fuel deterioration and the buildup of tank sludge.

3

Decontaminate

A water-block fine filter removes emulsified water and very fine solids.

The payoff

Benefits of professional fuel polishing.

  • Enhanced generator performance — cleaner fuel means more efficient combustion and better engine performance.
  • Extended equipment lifespan — less wear and tear on engine components, lowering maintenance costs.
  • Reduced downtime — minimizes the risk of generator failure during critical operations.
  • Cost savings — prevents costly repairs and fuel replacement expenses.
  • Environmental compliance — proper disposal of contaminants keeps you within environmental regulations.
