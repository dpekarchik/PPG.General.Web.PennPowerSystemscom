Our services

EGSA-certified

Technicians certified by the Electrical Generating Systems Association.

Our EGSA-certified power generation technicians are experts in the maintenance, repair, and operation of electrical power generation systems — a prestigious credential reflecting deep knowledge of diesel and gas engines, turbines, and renewable energy systems.

Certification covers a rigorous exam spanning electrical and mechanical theory, troubleshooting, system design, and safety. The result is reliability and efficiency where uninterrupted power is critical — healthcare, data centers, and industrial manufacturing.

What EGSA certification means:

  • Rigorous training and certification
  • Proven expertise in power generation
  • Commitment to industry standards
  • Access to cutting-edge tools and resources
  • Enhanced problem-solving capabilities
  • Continued professional development
  • Higher accountability and reliability
