The basics

What is loadbank testing?

Loadbank testing simulates electrical loads on power generation systems to verify their performance and reliability. Unlike passive testing — which only assesses operation under no-load conditions — loadbank testing applies varying levels of electrical load in a controlled environment, confirming your system can handle real-world demands.

Why it matters

Why loadbank testing is essential.

If you've ever weathered a power outage or brownout, you know how much rides on your emergency generator. Load bank testing makes sure your backup system functions properly when you need it.

  • Performance verification — confirms your system can handle its rated load capacity and run efficiently under varying conditions.
  • Preventive maintenance — surfaces potential issues before they become critical, reducing the risk of unexpected failures.
  • Compliance & certification — keeps you aligned with industry standards and regulations, maintaining certifications and licenses.
  • Optimal performance — simulating real-world load optimizes the performance and lifespan of your equipment.
What it covers

Our testing helps you:

  • Verify engine and generator performance
  • Determine cooling system efficiency
  • Satisfy NFPA and J.C.A.H.O. compliance requirements
  • Eliminate wet stacking
  • Increase system reliability
  • Evaluate voltage regulation and fuel response
