Power generators are critical assets that keep your operations running during outages. Whether industrial, commercial, or residential, reliability is paramount — and preventative maintenance is the key to maximizing the efficiency, longevity, and reliability of your generators. Penn Power Systems provides comprehensive preventative maintenance to keep your equipment in top condition.

Why it matters

Why preventative maintenance?

  • Reliability & peace of mind — we identify and fix issues before they cause costly breakdowns, so your generator is always ready.
  • Extended equipment lifespan — regular checks keep components in optimal condition, preventing premature wear.
  • Cost savings — addressing minor issues early reduces repair costs and minimizes downtime.
  • Improved efficiency — well-maintained generators use less fuel and deliver more consistent output.
  • Compliance & safety — routine maintenance keeps you within regulatory and safety standards, reducing risk.

Good to know

FAQs.

How often should preventative maintenance be performed?

It depends on usage and manufacturer recommendations — typically every 6 months or every 100 hours of operation.

What types of generators do you service?

A wide range — standby, portable, and industrial generators of all brands and sizes.

What happens if a problem is found during maintenance?

Our technicians provide a detailed report and discuss repair options so the issue is addressed promptly.

Can I perform maintenance myself?

Some basic tasks can be owner-done, but comprehensive maintenance should be performed by qualified professionals to ensure proper servicing and safety compliance.
