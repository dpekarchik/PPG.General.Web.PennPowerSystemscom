Privacy policy.

How Penn Power Systems collects, uses, and protects the information you share with us.

Last updated: January 2026

This policy explains what information Penn Power Systems collects when you use our website or services, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly — such as your name, company, email, phone number, and service details when you request a quote, submit a credit application, or contact us.

We also collect limited technical information automatically, such as your browser type, pages visited, and approximate location, to keep the site working and improve it.

How we use information

To respond to your requests, provide quotes and service, process credit and billing, and keep you informed about your equipment.

We do not sell your personal information.

Sharing

We share information only with service providers who help us operate (for example, payment processors and monitoring partners), and when required by law.

Your choices

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the information we hold about you, and you can opt out of marketing messages at any time.

Data security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission is completely secure, but we work to keep your data safe.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Call 1-877-PEN-POWR or reach us through our contact page.
