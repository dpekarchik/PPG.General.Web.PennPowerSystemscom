Towable and containerized rental power from 25 kW to 1 MW, dispatched from five regional centers with same-day availability for emergencies. Sales, rentals, service, parts — one phone call.

How rental works

Power on site, fast.

Planned shutdown, storm response, or a unit down without warning — the process is the same and it moves quickly.

1. Call us

Tell us your load, voltage, and timeline. We size the right unit on the phone.

2. We confirm

Availability from the nearest of five centers, with cabling and accessories included.

3. We deliver

Towable units arrive ready to connect — same-day for emergencies.

4. We support

Refueling, monitoring, and 24/7 backup for the length of the rental.

The fleet

From a single trailer to a paralleled bank.

Our rental fleet runs from compact towable units up to megawatt-class sets, with cabling, distribution, and transfer gear to match. If one unit can't carry the load, we parallel several.

  • 25 kW – 1 MW towable and containerized units
  • Cam-lock cabling, spider boxes, and distribution
  • Automatic transfer switch rentals
  • Refueling and remote monitoring add-ons
  • Short-term, monthly, and standby-contract terms
